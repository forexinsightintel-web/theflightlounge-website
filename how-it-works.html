The method

Cheap flights, with the receipts.

Most deal sites just shout "sale". We prove a price is good against real history — then, where it helps, we find it cheaper still and tell you the catch.

①

We track the fares

Every route from your home airports is priced four times a day and stored — timestamped from day one. Over time that builds an honest median: what this flight normally costs.

②

We weigh the verdict

A live fare is compared to that median. Meaningfully below and it's a Deal or a Steal; around normal and we say Typical — wait. No invented "was" prices, ever.

③

You book direct

We link you to the airline or a trusted booking site and you pay them — no markup, no mystery, no touching your card. A deal-finding club, not a travel agent.

Why "with the receipts" matters

Every saving we publish is our median minus the fare we spotted — a number you can audit, not a marketing figure. When we don't have enough history on a route to be sure, we say so rather than guess.

What we don't do

We don't sell flights, hold your payment, or take a cut of your booking. We don't invent a fake "was £499". And we never hide the catch on a clever route: if it's a self-transfer, you'll see the risk flag before you click.
