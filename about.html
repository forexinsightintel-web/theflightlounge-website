Who we are

A deal club that shows its working.

The travel-deal world runs on fake "was" prices and pressure. We built the opposite: every call weighed against real fare history, and the catch never hidden.

The problem we got tired of

Comparison sites bury the genuinely cheap fares under sponsored results, and "deal" newsletters shout about prices that were never really higher. You're left guessing whether £120 to Lisbon is a steal or a stitch-up. Nobody shows you the one thing that answers it: what that flight actually costs, most of the time.

What we do differently

We price every route from your airports continuously and keep the receipts, so we can put an honest verdict on any fare — Good Price, Typical or Wait — against a median we can prove. Then we go one better: we price every airport in your catchment and clever multi-leg routes, and only tell you when they genuinely win, with every risk flagged plainly. No markup, no payment handling — you always book direct with the airline.

The promise

Honest verdicts, real savings maths, and the catch up front. If a route is a self-transfer with a missed-connection risk, you'll know before you click. If we don't have enough history to be sure, we'll say so. That's the whole product.
