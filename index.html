Booking flights with The Flight Lounge

How does The Flight Lounge work?

We continuously track fares from your home airports, build a median price for every route, and surface the genuine drops. Each fare lands on your board with a plain verdict — GOOD PRICE, TYPICAL or WAIT — so you know it's actually cheap before you click. You book direct with the airline; we never touch your payment.

Why don't the verdicts show yet?

Because we refuse to call a price “good” off a thin sample. We're banking fare history now — once a route has enough, its verdict switches on automatically. Until then the prices are real and live; we just won't pretend to judge them yet.

Do you sell the flights?

No — and that's deliberate. We point you to the airline or a trusted booking site and you pay them directly, so there's no markup and no mystery. We're a deal-finding club, not a seller.
